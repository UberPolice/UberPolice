Description

Welcome to the Freddy Fazbear's Nightguard initiative! As you may have heard, due to a fire at the Mega Pizzaplex, all usable assets must be stored in a secure location until reconstruction can be completed. To cut costs of constructing a new building, Fazbear Entertainment has recently acquired the deed to an underground facility previously owned by a sister company. You will be posting as a new nightguard/technician. You will report to the building on 24/3/20## for your first day on the job. Wear that smile with pride. You are the NEW face of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza!

____________________________________________________________________

Die-hard Fnaf fans will be able to experience the world of Fnaf in a whole new light. Immerse yourself into the world of VR in this frightening fan game. Travel deep into the depths of the old Sister Location facility now owned by Fazbear Entertainment. Discover the company's secrets, gather tools and equipment. But above all, survive.
