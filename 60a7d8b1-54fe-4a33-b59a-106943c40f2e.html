Welcome to UNDERTALE YELLOW, a fan-made prequel to the hit RPG “UNDERTALE”

When a child named Clover sets out on a mission, they find themself deep within the Underground. In this foreign land, monsters lurk around every corner, but are they friend or foe?

In this full-length followup to the friendly RPG where nobody has to die, you decide how every encounter plays out, following the branching paths those decisions lead to. From unlikely allies to unlikely enemies, twists and turns will set Clover on a course towards an ultimate fate.

When is the full game releasing?

  • Now. You can play it :)

Will you make a game for -Insert soul color here- after UTY?

  • No, this game already drained all of our life force. Maybe someone else will!

Can you translate it into -insert language here-?

  • We currently have no concrete plans for translations. We understand that fans in other countries would like to enjoy it in their language but we only have an English version right now. However, if we do facilitate translations, Japanese and Spanish would be the top priority.

Did Toby greenlight this?

  • Kind of? But not quite. An email was sent to him before development began that basically said "Can we make an Undertale fangame?" to which he replied "Do what thou wilt."

Is this game a virus?

  • No. Windows may warn you that this game is suspicious but it is, in fact, quite spicious.

When will the game be ported to Mac?

  • We will work towards a macOS version in the future! In the meantime, there are options to run exe's on Mac.

Controller support?

  • Yes! UTY has full gamepad support for xinput controllers!

Accessibility options?

  • Yep, there are options to ease certain mechanics such as No Screenshake when you take damage, Auto-Fire to ease potential hand strain, Easy Mode to reduce damage taken, and Auto-Rhythm which simplifies a certain challenging boss fight.

Uploading content

  • When it comes to uploading content, we don’t like uploads that aren’t under fair use such as OST re-uploads as the full OST is already officially on YouTube. Extensions are passable as long as they aren’t monetized. Let’s Plays, remixes, animations, and anything else original are fully welcomed and encouraged though :)
