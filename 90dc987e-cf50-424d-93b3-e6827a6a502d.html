Recent update: 10/21/21

STORY

In the late '80s, a company that housed and produced advanced robotics for freelance use was very successful amongst the people. However, as time went by, the company's reputation began to fade with multiple rumors being spread about several events. Whether these were true or not is still up for debate.

This led to the shut down of their main housing facility.

Years later, the property was bought by a different, smaller, company that believes the building and its contents might have some use in this day and age. But, they are legally required to shut down the business if any disturbing evidence is found.

They are also looking for a night watchman, and YOU fit the criteria.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Windows XP or above

  • DirectX 9 or newer

  • A monitor with a minimum resolution of 1024x768

  • Minimum of 2GB Storage space

  • An NVIDIA GeForce 9400 or above. (This game uses heavy visual effects and may lag on older GPUs!)
