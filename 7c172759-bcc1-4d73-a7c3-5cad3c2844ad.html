MAIN INFORMATION

Conclusive Night with NeptooN - is a remake of old game One Night with NeptooN, containing new mechanics and a lot of features.

The story begins with the fact that you are hired as a night guard in a popular local restaurant "NeptooN's Planetarium". However, you later notice that animatronics do get bit quirky in this place, and behave like they have own personalities! How long can you survive this bizarre night shift?

FEATURES FROM THIS REMAKE

  • Improved graphics

  • Comical atmosphere

  • New characters' mechanics

  • Advanced gameplay

  • Escape section
