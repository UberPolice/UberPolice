Description

You're playing as a 16 year old girl - Bonnie Smith, who wants to find out the truth about her family. She was adopted by the last person in the Smith family (Edward Smith) in 2000. With time Edward told her about Fritz Smith and Hippo's Restaurant pizzeria, but he doesn't know everything. Three years have passed and Bonnie still wants to know the truth. That's why she decides to come to the abandoned and forgotten location of Hippo's Restaurant. But she wasn't expecting Fritz to still be alive

The game takes place in various locations related to Fritz's past, you'll have to find information about who he was. With time you will learn more about his past, you will find out what he had done, why Hippo's Restaurant was forgotten, and you'll get to know a dark secret of the Smith family

This game also shows the end of the deal with 'Snake'

When Freddy Fazbear's pizzeria was closed, a man appeared - Lucas Smith, who bought the restaurant from Fazbear Entertainment and repurposed it. He created Hippo's Restaurant with brand new animatronic characters, made from the Toy animatronics parts. It was going alright at first, but soon enough weird things started happening. There were no murders but there were kidnappings, which Lucas didn't know anything about. He could have never known that his son would become the second William Afton.

Fritz Smith is the main antagonist of the game. He used to be a normal person, but past incidents drove him to insanity... You are trying to find out the truth about him. He had once committed crimes as a human and, as punishment, he became a victim of a trap he had created. However, he is still alive - his body might be dead but his brain is connected to the AI, developed by himself.
