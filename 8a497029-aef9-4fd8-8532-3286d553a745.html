Description

Zolly Industrial Madness is a FNaF Fan-Game as the start of the revival of the original Zolly Series that I Made back in 2020 and through the beginning of 2021, Until during the summer of 2021, Canceled the games and moved on until I thought of a better way to start over with this series. But here we are now!

You play as the role of Izzy Watson, A Machinist at the factory of Cybermatics Inc
After two months of progress of the making of the new technology for the brand new Zolly Animatronics for a Brand new location that is opening in the next weeks called “Zolly’s Wonder Land”

The CEO of Cybermatics Inc Gabriel Bryant, Has called Izzy up for a task for the week to stay at the facility overnight to be a security guard around 12AM all the way to 6AM. Will the shift be safe over the week? Or will the hacked animatronics be up all night haunting you.
