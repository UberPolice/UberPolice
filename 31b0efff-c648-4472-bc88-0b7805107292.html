Synopsis

"Welcome to Claustrophobic Nights; a fun virtual interactive experience! At Fazbear Entertainment, your comfort and emotional well being is our top priority. That's why you will find yourself locked in a dark room, alone, and surrounded by vents. But watch out, the animatronic mascots tend to wander, and may attempt to express their affection in an overwhelming and deadly fashion. So be careful, stay alert, and most of all, have fun!"

About The Game

Claustrophobic Nights is a complete reimagining of Crawlspace 2 in the style of FNaF Help Wanted with multiplayer included. In the game, you crawl through tight spaces and solve various puzzles whilst evading animatronics in order to beat the night.

Features

  • Multiplayer with up to 5 Players in a Lobby

  • 7 Main Nights and Custom Night with Challenges

  • 3 Different Maps to choose from, each with their Own Unique Spins (5 more to come in update 1.2)

  • Over 10 Different Enemies to encounter (20+ to come in update 1.2)

  • An Interactive Tutorial
