After a horde of flames has consumed you whole, all you see is black, then white, then nothing, and suddenly you're back, but, something isn't right. "Wasn't I just in that god forsaken horror attraction?"

You have no way of knowing what's down here, so you head to the most secure looking room, two exits, and a window behind, and you sit long ways from the computer that greets you at the door, and suddenly, bells, their slumber is over and an obscured phone starts ringing.

Good luck.

Dormitabis Remastered is a FNaF fangame that takes place shortly after FNaF3 where the good ending is not canon and therefore is not related to any FNaF games past FNaF SL.

It follows its own canon that works off of the basis of FNaF3's fire, with Springtrap dying shortly after it.

With a standard amount of deaths the game can take about 2 hours to beat in a standard session, even longer if playing casually.

to 100% is also WAY longer as there are secret areas/minigames and other challenges that give you special rewards as well.

Content this game has that original does not.

  • A fully original soundtrack

  • A fully functional custom night mode

  • More animations (and better animations)

  • DLCs based on the 2018, and 2019 version of the game

  • More secret endings and easter eggs
