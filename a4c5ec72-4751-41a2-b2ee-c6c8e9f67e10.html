A CLOISTER HIDDEN FROM PRYING EYES

FFAF is a fan spinoff in a series of horror games for the whole family, which have gained popularity with millions of players around the world. You have to fit into a broken code, a hidden abode and find a way out before they find you.

MANY VICTIMS. You have to assemble a puzzle and figure out the dark history of Fazbear Entertainment, get acquainted with the villains and decide their fate. Everything is in your hands.

ADAPT TO THE ALIEN WORLD. Find hidden objects, solve riddles and follow the story. Take care of the flashlight, escape from the "madness" and old friends of Freddy Fazber from the previous parts of the game franchise!

EXPLORE AND THINK. You are not alone here and you will be disturbed. Try to stay alive and avoid the dangers. Freddy and his friends never sleep. The dark part of Fazbear Entertainment is truly huge - there are a lot of interesting finds waiting for you here!
