Description

STORY

Miles Ahead Storage & Distribution, Inc. is a place located in Oxford, UK. A warehouse that has been keeping numerous items from the Fredbear and Friends establishment safe, until one late night.

Several days following the company's radio silence, you, in search of a missing friend decide to give the warehouse a little visit, but things aren't as they seem.

You don't have much time before they get to you.

ABOUT

[The Fredbear Archives] is a short horror-themed arcade experience based on the Five Nights at Freddy's saga, and is a spin-off to the Fredbear and Friends series. The main objective of the game is to escape by completing certain tasks and solving puzzles in a limited period of time.

FEATURES:

  • Multiple ways to escape

  • Full free-roam

  • Cutscenes

  • Trophies

  • Puzzles

WARNING

A powerful gaming computer is required to play the game on higher graphical settings.

This game has been tested at a stable 150 - 200 frames per second with the following specifications:

  • OS: Windows 10 Home 64-bit

  • Graphics Card: Nvidia GTX 1050

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-7400 CPU @ 3.00GHz (4 CPUs)

  • Memory: 8GB RAM
