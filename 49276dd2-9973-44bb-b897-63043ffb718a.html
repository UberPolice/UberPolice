Description

"Uncover the secrets of Fredbear's Family Diner..."

In the aftermath of a tragic incident, the notorious diner remains open against all odds. Henry Emily and William Afton persist in their relentless work, striving to keep the restaurant afloat. As you step into the shoes of John Hudson, a father recently laid off, your primary concern becomes providing for your family. Rumors circulate about a potential job opening at "Fredbear's Family Diner," urging you to seize the opportunity before it slips away. Little do you know the unforeseen consequences that await you as you unknowingly become entangled in a dark and twisted game orchestrated by two vengeful souls. The animatronics, once innocent and joyful, have been reprogrammed. Night after night, you must navigate the haunted halls, surviving and unraveling the enigmatic secrets concealed within the restaurant's walls...

Night Shift

		
			This game features gameplay inspired by "Five Nights at Freddy's", but with its own distinct mechanics.

Post-Nights

		
			Engage in additional activities that are available after completing the main nights.

Minigames

		
			A fresh take on the classic "8-bit minigames" from the original game.
