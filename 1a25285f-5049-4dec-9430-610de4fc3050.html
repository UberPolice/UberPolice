———————— Birthday at Freddy's - A FNAF Fan Game ————————

    Links: YouTube — Trailer — Twitter — Discord

Birthday at Freddy's is based on the legendary FNaF game by Scott Cawthon. The game takes place in a godforsaken restaurant whose history is written in blood. You have to unravel the mysteries of this place, along the way, beware of the animatronics, thirsty for your death. But is it worth fearing someone else besides these soulless animatronics?
