NEO SUPER SCALER RACING

Drift your way to the finish line in this fast paced arcade style racer with super scaler aesthetics!

Gameplay Features

  •  F A S T  paced, over the top Arcade style gameplay

  • Breakneck speeds, drifts & stunts

  • Tight & responsive controls

  • 2 playable characters/cars with unique stats

  • Multiple game modes like arcade, time attack & more!

  • Gorgeous hand made pixel art & animation by Skydevilpalm

  • Banging soundtrack by RoBKTA & GWIZ
