Multiplayer?

This game probably won't have realtime multiplayer, at least not now. Maybe in the future. For now, the planned multiplayer means just an integration between players, and their pizzerias. It's not the type of multiplayer where you can see everybody else at realtime. Not yet.

There isn't anything specific yet.

  • A stable internet connection is required in order to play the game.

If the realtime multiplayer will be added, also Steam, logged in and running in the background. If it never happens, the game could maybe even get released on Android one day. But these are just my "loud thoughts".
