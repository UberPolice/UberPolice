Description

In this game you can create your own maps and share them with your friends or with the whole world. The editor has a huge number of tiles, decorations and NPCs. The game also has 3 game modes - CLASSIC, CHASING and MASK. Among other things, the game also has triggers that make the gameplay more fun =)

The game also has an animatronics editor. in it, unsurprisingly, you can create animatronics to your liking. If this is not enough for you, then the animatronics editor also has a choice of abilities and extra abilities. what are abilities?

Abilities are what your animatronic will be able to do, for example: Super-speed, teleportation, reducing anxiety to zero, speeding up time, etc. however, abilities have one minus, they have cooldown.

Extra abilities are abilities that have no cooldown and are always active. Extra abilities and abilities can be used at the same time

The game supports GameJolt API, which means that you can get Scores and Trophies
