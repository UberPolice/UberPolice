<><> What is Five Nights at Freddy's: The Deluxe Collection? <><>

Five Nights at Freddy's: The Deluxe Collection (FNaF DX) is a fan-made project that aims to upgrade & optimize the first 4 Five Nights at Freddy's games. These games are completely reprogrammed from the ground up!
