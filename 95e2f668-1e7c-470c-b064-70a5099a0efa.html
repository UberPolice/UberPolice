In this realtime multiplayer game, you, along with a few buddies, will relive the nightmares of the forgotten pizzeria - together! You could even become the animatronic this time…

The action takes place at night, in the insanely big, randomly generated Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Players are randomly placed in one of the two available categories: nightguards and animatronics.

Guards rush to find tools to complete maintenance work, while the animatronics hunt them down with their distinct, special abilities.

FAQ:

  • Q: Is this a virus? Windows defender doesn't want to let me open it.
    A: No, it just isn't verified as something trustworthy in Windows's databases, so it will be cautious about it, but don't worry, there's nothing that can do any harm to your computer in it, 100%. Click 'more info' and 'run anyway'.

  • Q: I have a .zip file. What do I do now? How do I install?
    A: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eZOOQwPDvZQ - do what he does with your .zip file. Don't move the .exe out of the folder, if you want it on your desktop, make a shortcut and that can be moved around freely.

    Alternatively, if you can't figure that out, you can also install a Gamejolt client and launch directly from there without worries ^^

  • Q: it won't let me log in with Gamejolt... I input everything correctly, yet it doesn't work. What do I do?
    A: Make sure you're using a Game Token, not your account password. Find it in an upper-right menu on gamejolt, in "game token" section.

  • Q: Will you give nightguards a minimap?
    A: I might in the future. Carrying it will surely be a tradeoff for precious inventory space though.

  • Q: Are updates coming? If they are, when?
    A: The game is being worked on in my free time, inconsistently since I also now have some rathermore original projects going. So yeah, they are, but there's no schedule and no obligations on my side.

  • Q: I found a bug, how do I report it?
    A: Join a Discord server, and describe the bug to me, with details.

  • Q: What are the console commands?
    A: use the 'help' command in the ingame console to get a small article with an explanation.

  • Q: Will you make a mobile build?
    A: No. The game uses a PC - only Steam networking, as well as it's just too high-end with its graphics. You phone would fry, not even talking about an absolute inability to join or create servers.
