Synopsis:

Ideas of the post-death world are ambiguous. It could bring you anywhere, it could be anything. Rumors vary from one person to another, but Robin is about to discover the truth... Memories clash as he finds himself somewhere familiar. Guided through purgatory by helpful spirit Spectri, The CyberPlex opens its doors. Uncover the secrets of this strange but all too familiar world before it’s too late.

Gameplay (Currently in Production):

Explore the hidden depths of the Cyberplex, encompassed within a unique and riveting UCN-styled gameplay environment. Counter against an entourage of threats now planning your demise, will you last another day in Purgatory?
