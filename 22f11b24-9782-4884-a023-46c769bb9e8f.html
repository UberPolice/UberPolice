Description

Overheat is an arcade racing game inspired by: Need for speed Hot pursuit, Test Drive unlimited 2 and Midnight Club LA. You can personalize your cars according to your taste and improve the performance of your vehicle the higher your level is. You can also unlock weapons for your cars such as spikes, emp, etc.
