Description

The year is 1996, after the closure of their previous location, R. Animatronics Co. wanted to make their next chance a good one. Starting with new and advanced animatronics to entertain for the likes of many. Random’s Pizza Time Theater would become the choice of many for events, because of the animatronics almost human like personalities, they thought that was always how it had been, but something more sinister had happened behind the scenes...

You play as Brooke Ares, a security guard hired by R. Animatronics Co. to make sure nothing happens to these robots. But as you sit and stare, waiting for the end of the week, you start to realize these characters know more than you think. From 11 PM to 6 AM you work the night shift in order to gain that sweet, sweet, dough.
