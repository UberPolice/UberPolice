Description

This game is for those 18 years or older, as this game contains extremely vulgar language, sexual references and sensitive themes.

Description:
You have been called out by a green animatronic rabbit with glasses, his name is 39.
He has a strange personality, something you wouldn't see in your average animatronic, he's foul, he's roudy, he's stupid, he's complicated, and he's coming for you!

Survive his random movements, and his pal, Plushie, both who will surely end you if you are not quick enough, boop both of their noses to keep them away!
(For whatever reason, they do not like it.)

Features:

  • Eight playable nights.

  • A complex, lovable and hateable character and his buddy.

  • A mysteriously convuluted story.

  • 16 Obtainable GameJolt Trophies.

This game was born out of a random little challenge I made for myself, and that, in my opinion, is not my best work, but it still blew up...

This game would not have Night 7, Custom Night or any of its updates and sequels, if it wasn't for you guys.
