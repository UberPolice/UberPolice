Description

The year is 2023, after many years of being open "Chica's Party World" is preparing for a major renovation! But with any high-end establishment, a form of protection is necessary. This is where you come in!

Upon being found in storage, you've been re-activated with the task of keeping the place guarded. Monitor cameras, patrol the premises, and most importantly complete the tasks you've been provided. But... why? Wait, why are you questioning it? It's your duty, so get to work. Now.

"Will you break the system? Or will it break you?"
