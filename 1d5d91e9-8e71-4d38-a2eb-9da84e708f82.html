Description

Jolly: ERROR serves as an unnoficial spinoff game of the JOLLY Series. This shall feature reoccuring characters such as George the Tiger and Maxie the Cat, Tweetie the Bird, and the one and only, Jolly the Bee! and more.

5 days after the closure of Jolly's Fantasy Land, the animatronics have been decommisioned for parts for the next Generation of animatronics, also known as the vintages. Unfortunately, these characters looked too similar to the cancelled animatronic designs from older locations, so they decided to scrap them and ship them to the JOLLY Entertainment storage facility to be watched until they are shipped to the new location to be used for parts.

Jeremy, the character you will be playing as, is to watch the animatronics for a full week. Unfortunately for him, they were left on for no apparent reason, and they are out for blood.
