Description

Project Readjusted 3 is an indie horror game inspired by #fnaf and the final entry in the Project Readjusted trilogy. It is rendered in Blender 2.79 (Internal) and programmed in Clickteam Fusion 2.5+. You are driven to collect the pieces revolving around the man of mystery, Matthew ▒▒▒▒▒, and find yourself taking up a deal you know you could not pass upon. Little did you know, this deal has a dark catch.
