Story:

“Brady & Friends Pizzeria, huh? It’s been a while since Gorman Robotics ever announced anything about building a new attraction, especially after what happened back in 1975. Yesterday I decided to pay a visit to this new establishment, looking pretty innocent from the looks of things. Kids were cheering, the animatronics singing and dancing away, pretty normal stuff. The day after I got a newspaper in the mail, reading up on an article saying they require a security guard to watch over the building during the week. Since I needed a new job anyways, I gave them a call and signed up for the job. That day was known to be the worst choice I have ever made.”
