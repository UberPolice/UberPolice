Description

Five Nights at Freddy's: Bloodshed is a multiplayer, free roam PvP FNaF FANGAME where players choose their characters, and fight with each other.

Every character from the franchise will get their own abilities that you will use to fight.

THIS IS EARLY, EXPERIMENTAL ALPHA/BETA VERSION/STATE OF THE GAME.

REPORT ALL OF THE BUGS ON OUR DISCORD SERVER!

Before playing, make sure you have a decent, modern PC. This game could be a challenge for your PC, because it's fully 3D real-time rendered game.

I don't think that you will be able to play this game on 2011 laptop.

The game currently has 3 Gamemodes. Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, and Remnant Madness.

For the first final game version, there will be 5 (FNaF 1) characters, but I am also planning updating the game, with new characters, maps, modes and other features in a (far) future.

There is probably also coming Story mode, but it's not even coming for the final release. Maybe like 2.0 version.

The game is being made in Unity 2021, using mirror-networking and FizzySteamworks.

There will never be any dedicated servers from my side. You have to host the servers on your own.

  https://trello.com/b/Z2YwMXTQ/…

Check how far the next update is!

Game runs with Steam API as Spacewar - you need a Steam account.

What is Spacewar? (Maybe I'm not even right)

Spacewar is some kind of a test app provided by Steam. It is some kind of an example of possibilities and functionalities that are (I guess?) dedicated to game developers.

Unfortunately, Spacewar is also used for game piracy. Even if... it doesn't mean my game is some kind of a pirated game... Anyway... some people might think that you are playing pirated games if they will see Spacewar on your Steam profile.

I suggest creating a new Steam account if you want to be sure that nothing like this will happen. But people that would accuse you for game pirating are just ignorants and people that have no knowledge about Steam API and/or Spacewar.

What about VAC Ban?

VAC Ban is Valve Anti Cheat Ban - Spacewar isn't a game cheat.

I did never hear about any case of VAC Ban because of Spacewar, but if you are really afraid of that, you can make a new Steam account, just for the game.

But... I use Spacewar for many hours with my game, and I'm not banned. Same as my friends.

So I don't think there is anything to worry about.

But it's your choice. You can use your main account, make a new one, or just don't play my game at all if you are afraid of its safety.
