Description

You are an unemployed college junior who is strapped for cash. While browsing the Yellow Pages, you notice an ad for a night watchman position at Hugo's Hole in the Wall. It looks simple enough, and you could definitely use the money. You decide to give them a call and see when you can start.

Little do you know what awaits...

  • 3D FNAF-style gameplay

    • Close doors, browse monitors, and shock foxes to survive until 6 AM.

  • 6 exciting hours

    • Hours are similar to nights in other FNAF games, but the game saves after every hour, and they are each 6 real life minutes.

  • 10+ challenges

    • Play through a variety of unique challenges that mix-up the traditional gameplay!

  • Office customization

    • Fully customize your office with unlockable decorations, trophies, and posters!

    • You can even upload your own images to use as posters!

  • Achievements

  • Secrets and lore

  • A mode that is six hours long in real-life

  • Animatronic costumes and skins

  • 5+ lovable animatronics

    • Animatronics use pathfinding and physically move around the location in real time.

    • Use camera sirens to affect their behavior!

Six Hours at Hugo's is a 3D first-person horror game developed in Godot 4, with 3D Objects that are modeled in Blender.
