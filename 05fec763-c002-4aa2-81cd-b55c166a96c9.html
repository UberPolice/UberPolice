#Description and Instructions

#AdvJam2018

#Update - We polished and improved the game for Adventure Jam. The file upload got cancelled twice and took forever. Our update is available and ready to play now!

Abandoned train station, rats transforming into monsters. Move with WASD or Arrow keys. Shoot with Left Mouse button or Punch with Right Mouse button. Kill the rats and absorb their souls to heal yourself and absorb their mutation. Unlock the leap ability and press and hold Q, jump, let go of Q to leap mid-air. Unlock the spit ability and press F to fire an explosive acid ball once in 5 seconds.
Find the vents spawning the rats and close them with E - when you have enough souls. Watch out for the green poison rats which can be attacking you from a distance.
This was created in 1 week.
