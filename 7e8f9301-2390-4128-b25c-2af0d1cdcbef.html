Description

! WARNING !

This game is a love letter to the "Five Nights at Sonic's" fnaf fangame series, but also pokes a lot of fun at negative topics revolving around its community. Remember, that this is all made for fun and is in no way is trying to genuinely insult anyone.

Don't take it personal.

Also this is not an official FNAS game, I put "Official" so there is no confusion if people reupload this game.

1 year after the closing of the first Sonic's Hero Pizza Palace, the brand decides to prepare for a second establishment. In the meantime of their planning, the clones are put into a warehouse far away somewhere in Green Hill Zone called the: "Sonic's Hero Pizza Palace Archives" for shelter and private birthday rentals. You play as Knuckles the Echidna, the security guard of said warehouse. Watch the clones and make sure they like their new home!

Don't expect this to be as easy as the last location, they have new plans and are more bloodthirsty than before.
