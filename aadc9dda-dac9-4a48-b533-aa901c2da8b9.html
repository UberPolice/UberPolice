Description

Wave Shock Is a fangame of the game Case 2 Animatron Survival.

- Case Animatron belongs to : WALNUT (OOO VALNAT)

Please also Support the Devs of the main game: CASE: Animatronics , Walnut LLC or https://www.walnutdevgames.com/?lang=en .

Controls:
Move - WASD
Shoot - LMB
Zoom - RMB
Reload - R
Run - SHIFT
Pause - ESC
Flashlight - F
Abilitys - 1,2,3
Interact - E
Change Weapon - Tab, Mousewheel
Crouch - CTRG
Jump (only Last Retar) - 2,SPACE
Perspective - V
Change CCTV - W,S

Story:

You've been battling the bots for weeks and during that time you've met 2 other survivors. They team up and battle the bots a second time.

In the meantime, Vise has bought more useful bots that want to kill you.

How long can You Survive?
