It has been one year since the horrific shooting at the Gilbert Facility hospital, an event which left dozens dead, and many more left critically injured.
In spite of political controversy and public outcry, the Gilbert Facility’s board of directors has decided to reopen and continue its secretive operations. That is, after a slight renovation.
You, Loyd, have been hired for the job of nighttime security guard. Being given and trained in the use of multiple special tools, your job is to observe the decrepit building, prevent anyone or anything from getting in, and keeping a close eye on the hospital’s hidden projects.

However, an unknown force has other plans…

_RADSLA_ REMAKE is a recreation of the game with a combination of old and new assets and is an attempt at finishing what could've been an actual RADSLA game, if

@TylerAhlstrom
hadn't, unfortunately, canceled it.

_RADSLA_ REMAKE features *almost* everything that's been planned for the original game and more!
