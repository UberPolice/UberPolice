Description

O’Relic is a Five Nights at Freddy’s fan game that takes the original FNaF story and expands on it. Multiple different playthroughs of the game can end with several different endings, all equally canon to the O’Relic lore and tie in to the official FNaF storyline (still unofficial, of course). Soon after the full game release, there will be 3 separate smaller games expanding the world of O’Relic with new modes of gameplay, story, and characters.

O’Relic is a strategy/horror game shrouded with mystery. The small town of Hurricane, Utah has a blanket of hysteria covering its population. Dustin Falce, a highschooler and citizen of this small community, finds himself in the middle of a conflict regarding his uncle Thomas O’Relic’s dark legacy. It’s said Thomas locked away his own family in an unknown location decades ago, and was never seen again. Can Dustin navigate through his normal life while discovering the secrets of O’Relic with the help of both friends and strangers?

During the events of O’Relic, Dustin can find himself in many different areas and situations, where many varying mechanics and actions may be necessary for his safety! There are three main gameplay settings that Dustin has the choice to visit each daily, the O’Relic Family Corner, Hurricane Highschool, and of course, his own house. Who knows what may cause unsafe situations in these places? Every time Dustin visits the O’Relic Family Corner, he receives a paycheck from his… boss. Dustin can visit the nearby convenience store anytime to purchase items to assist him whenever he needs it, or he could just pick up a few cool little knick knacks.

Dustin doesn’t need to do any of this though. He has the option to stay home the whole day and do nothing, which of course brings other problems into play. It’s up to the player what he will choose; every action has a consequence, good or bad. These choices can lead to multiple endings, each of them equally canon to the story. Treacherous new areas and scandalous secrets will be revealed! Can you unlock them all and piece together the mystery of the O’Relic family?
