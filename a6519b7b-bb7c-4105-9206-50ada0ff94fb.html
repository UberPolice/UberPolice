Welcome to Cakebear's, a place where fun and fantasy came to life before it was closed down temporarily. A manager went missing in the area and the evidence points to this place being the last known place he was seen, which is why we're here. According to our reports, there have been sightings of figures and faces at the windows and items being misplaced.

We need to crack down on this case.

Cakebear's is a Five Nights at Freddy's-inspired game made as a collaboration effort between The Classy Baryonyx and Dr. Cross, who are keen on bringing a new horror experience into the fangame scene. In this game, you will be spending a number of nights at the diner, fending off aggressive mascot characters, while ignoring any potential passive distractions.

Utilise your cameras, EMF, Switch Board and hiding places appropriately against the catalysts of the nightmarish situation you are placed in. Look for details that can help you differentiate the evil from the good. Use your skills and knowledge of what's going on to pull the plug on this horror show.
