LUMINANCE DISCORD

STORY

Welcome to Rat Race!

You, one of four lucky contestants, have been chosen to compete in a game of cat-and-mouse, except you take on BOTH roles! Defend yourself against an onslaught of robots sent by the other competitors, whilst you send out your own army. The last man standing will be the winner!

Gear up, lucky four, and let the games begin!

GAMEPLAY

Rat Race is a frantic, more tactical multiplayer game. Your wits and quick-thinking will be your best friends here as you manage offense and defense.

  • Four animatronics per team, four offices, and four players! With 20+ rooms, this game is bound to keep you on your toes.

  • Sabotage your opponents' equipment or animatronics, while fighting off attempted hacks!

  • Upgrade your characters' capabilities through skill trees. Be careful with your choices!

  • All hosted on private, secure servers with custom anti-cheat measures and cloud-based progress saving! Online leaderboards, GameJolt integration, Discord Rich Presence, and minor modding supported.
