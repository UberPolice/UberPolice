Hello and welcome to the world of Animatronica! Meet new friends and... Wait what? Animatronica is under attack! Glitches are turning Animatronica into a wasteland and causing the animatronics to go rouge. Freddy and Co. will have to rise to the rescue to fend off Animatronica once again!

FNaF World Redux is a remaster of FNaF World with extras features like:

- New characters to play as

- New zones to explore

- New enemies to fight

- New ending to complete

- Gamejolt API connection so you can link your trophies to your gamejolt account
