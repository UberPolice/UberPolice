Description

Rev up your engines in BoxSoft's newest game, Faz-Karts! Hop into a server with up to 7 of your friends, and race against them with a variety of FNaF's most iconic characters!

Join our Discord Server for updates on what's to come!

Features:

  • Eight characters with differing stats, with more always just around the corner!

  • Selectable skins of the characters to transform them into recognizable variants like Blacklights, Gold Chrome, or even FNaF AR Skins!

  • Three maps remixing Freddy Fazbear's from FNaF1 & FNaF2, and Fazbear's Frights, with more also just around the corner!

  • Collect as many Faz-Coins as you can on the courses, while picking up any items you see along the way to try and sabotage any other racers so you finish in first place!

Controls:

  • WASD / Right Buttons - Drive

  • Space / RB - Drift

  • ALT / Left Down Button - Look Behind You

  • SHIFT / LB - Use Item

  • ESC / START - Pause
